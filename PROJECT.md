# EElat Website

Official website for the EElat music project.

---

## Vision

A cinematic, minimal and artistic website focused on motion, atmosphere and sound rather than heavy graphics.

The experience should feel timeless, mysterious and immersive.

## Hero Direction

No photographs.

The hero should rely on:

- abstract animated shapes
- particles
- gradients
- light
- depth
- cinematic motion
- premium typography

Reference feeling:

Arrival

Dune

Blade Runner 2049

Interstellar

without copying any of them.

## Animation Rules

Animations must be subtle.

Avoid flashy effects.

Everything should feel calm and expensive.

Preferred:

- opacity
- blur
- scale
- slow rotation
- floating motion
- parallax
- stagger

Avoid:

- bouncing
- spinning logos
- flashy transitions
