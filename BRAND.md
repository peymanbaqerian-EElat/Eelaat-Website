# Brand Guide

## Visual Identity

EElat should feel minimal, cinematic, dark, timeless, and premium. The experience should suggest depth, mood, and refinement rather than clutter or novelty.

## Core Principles

- Minimal
- Cinematic
- Dark
- Timeless
- Premium
- Organic Motion
- Abstract Shapes
- Elegant Serif Typography
- Soft Violet Accent
- No Stock Photos
- Motion First
- Atmosphere Before Information

## Expression

The brand should be expressed through slow movement, thoughtful pacing, and carefully controlled contrast. Visual storytelling should feel intimate, immersive, and quietly luxurious.

## Typography

Use elegant serif typography for hero and section titles, with a refined supporting type system for body content. Prioritize clarity, spacing, and a sense of calm confidence.

## Color Direction

- Background: near-black or deep charcoal
- Text: warm off-white
- Accent: soft violet or muted lavender
- Supporting tones: muted gray and subtle shadows

## Visual Language

Avoid literal photography when possible. Favor abstract composition, atmosphere, texture, and motion. The site should feel authored rather than generic.
