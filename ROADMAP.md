# Roadmap

## Phase 1 Foundation

- Finalize the visual direction and page architecture
- Build the core layout for hero, about, music, gallery, and contact
- Establish styling conventions, spacing, and typography rules
- Ensure the site is responsive and fast on first load

## Phase 2 Experience

- Refine motion and transitions throughout the interface
- Improve page storytelling with richer section flow and atmosphere
- Polish interaction details for hover, scroll, and reveal moments
- Introduce better content hierarchy and visual rhythm

## Phase 3 Content

- Add final copy for each section
- Expand music-related content and presentation
- Integrate gallery content and supporting media
- Improve storytelling around the brand, sound, and identity

## Phase 4 Release

- Optimize performance, accessibility, and SEO
- Review the experience across devices and browsers
- Prepare deployment, analytics, and final launch checks
- Publish the site and continue iterative refinement
